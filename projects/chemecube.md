The purpose of the project is to build a chemical plant within a cubic foot that performs direct air capture (DAC) for the 2026 ChemE Cube competition. For this competition we must also present our design in front of a panel of judges, create a technical poster detailing our design, produce a 1-minute video advertisement, and design a custom logo. 

As a co-founder of this team and a leading member, my role is to plan the project with our 2 co-leads, lead the administration and marketing teams, ensure that our 15 members are on track to meet the competition deadlines, ensure that our team will qualify, help with training members, and assist with the design (primarily the electrical/electronics components). 

Before we qualify for the main competition in Minneapolis, MN, hosted at the annual AIChE Student Conference, we have to present our design in early April. Right now the team's focus is solidifying our design and producing it in CAD using OnShape.

It's been a lot of work, but I've been having a blast seeing this project come to fruition over the past couple of weeks and seeing how much the team has grown from just an idea in my head to a functional and passionate group of people working on real-world problems. I'm really excited to see it come together and am confident our team has what it takes to excel in this competition!
